ClipVortex Installation Guide

Welcome to the ClipVortex Installation Guide. Follow the steps below to get started with our clipboard manager.

Step 1: Download

Visit the ClipVortex website and choose the appropriate version for your device.

Download ClipVortex

Step 2: Install

Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

Step 3: Setup

Once installed, open ClipVortex and set up your preferences, including syncing your clipboard data.
